About BUSSSA

Our Mission

To work together as a team to ensure that the needs of South Sudanese students are met through equitable access to appropriate learning and life skills programs to improve various aspects of quality education, social, management and excellence of all, in order to be recognized as measurable learning University students

Our Vision

To be a leading student organization that empowers South Sudanese & Sudanese students to excel academically and culturally.
